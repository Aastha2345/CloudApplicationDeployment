About Us

What is UPES Sensei?

UPES Sensei is a platform to support the learning and academic experience of students. The goal is to make education accessible and affordable for everyone, regardless of their location or schedule.


What it Offer

The platform includes several sections such as a notes section, home page, doubts section, and announcement section. The notes section contains notes for every academic year, which students can access and download to support their learning. The home page serves as a central hub for students to access all of the features of the platform and get updates on important university news and events. The doubts section allows students to ask questions and receive assistance from their professors and peers, while the announcement section provides regular updates on academic schedules, examination dates, and other important information.


Why UPES Sensei

  1. Flexible and self-paced learning
  2. Expert instructors with industry experience
  3. Real-world projects and hands-on exercises
  4. Affordable and accessible education
  5. 24/7 support and assistance
